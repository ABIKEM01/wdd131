About Me

My name is Abiodun Temitope Babayode, a Nigerian software developer with a strong background in both food technology and computer science. Lagos, Nigeria

Lagos is Nigeria's largest city and the economic hub of West Africa. Known as the "Centre of Excellence," it's a vibrant metropolis that blends traditional Nigerian culture with modern urban development. The city is famous for its thriving tech ecosystem, diverse population of over 15 million people, and as the heart of Nollywood, Nigeria's film industry. Lagos is also a major financial center and the gateway to Nigeria's economy.
