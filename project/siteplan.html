Servver Website Plan

A service booking platform for Nigeria

Site Name

Servver – This name represents the service booking application that connects people in Nigeria with reliable service workers for home, car, and personal services. I chose it because it is short, memorable, and directly tied to the app brand I am developing.

Optional Domain: servver.ng

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Servver website is to provide information about the mobile application, including its benefits, available service categories, and how users can book service providers. It also serves as a contact point for customer inquiries and support. The website will showcase the value of the app and help build trust with potential users.

Scenarios

  • “How can I book a service provider for plumbing repairs?”
  • “What types of services are available through Servver?”
  • “Where can I download the app or get customer support?”

Color Scheme

The website will use a professional and trustworthy color palette.

  • Deep Blue (#0A2540): Used for headers, navigation, and accents to symbolize trust and professionalism.
  • Light Gray (#F5F5F5): Used as background for sections to keep the design clean and easy to read.
  • Accent Orange (#FC8B00): Used for call-to-action buttons and highlights to attract user attention.

Typography

  • Headings: "Poppins", sans-serif – clean, modern, and professional.
  • Body Text: "Roboto", sans-serif – highly readable for paragraphs and descriptions.
  • Special Callouts: "Montserrat", sans-serif – for buttons and emphasis text.
